Winni-Furance Score Mechanism

How You Can Earn Scores

Note : Only registered website members can earn scores and enjoy the benefits.

  • Sign Up on the Website

    Upon signing up on the website and confirming your email address, you will receive +5 Scores.

  • Sign Up with a Referral Invite Link

    If you sign up on the website using a referral invite link and confirm your email address, you will receive +10 Scores.

  • Order a Service from the Website

    When you order a service from the website, and we complete your order, you will receive +10 Scores.

  • Share Your Unique Referral Link

    After confirming your email, you will receive a unique invite link in your dashboard. For every user who signs up and confirms their email through your link, you will earn +5 Scores. Share your unique invite link on social media and email from your dashboard.

How You Can Use Your Scores

Redeem Your Scores for Discount Codes

Accumulate 100 Scores, and you can redeem them for a discount code, providing you with a 10% Discount on your next order. Website members enjoy an additional 10% Discount. Once you reach 100 Scores, you can request a new, unique discount code in your dashboard.
