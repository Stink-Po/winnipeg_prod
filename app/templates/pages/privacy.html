Privacy Policy

Welcome to Winni Furnace, your trusted local HVAC service provider in Winnipeg, Manitoba. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information

    • When you sign up on our website, we collect your email address, phone number, and optional address information for communication and service delivery.

    • When send a message with contact us form on our website, we collect your email address, phone number, and optional address information for communication and service delivery.

    • When order a service on our website, we collect your email address, phone number, and optional address information for communication and service delivery.

  2. Usage Data

    • We may collect information about how you interact with our website, such as the pages visited and actions taken.

Use of Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  1. Account Management:

    • To create and manage your account, providing you with personalized services.

  2. Communication:

    • To contact you regarding your account, service updates, or promotions.

  3. Service Delivery:

    • To fulfill HVAC service requests and improve our services.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. We employ industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

User Control

  1. Account Deletion

    • Users have the right to delete their accounts at any time. Upon deletion, all associated information will be permanently removed from our database.

  2. Opt-Out:

    • Users can opt-out of promotional communications by following the instructions provided in the emails.
